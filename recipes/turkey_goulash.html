Turkey Goulash

Description

Small tweaks to the classic goulash, to make it lighter. Recommended to be served with a salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground turkey
  • 1 (14 ounce) can stewed, diced tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomatoe sauce
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 (16 ounce) package bow tie pasta

Steps

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook turkey until browned.
  2. Stir in the stewed tomatoes, garlic, tomatoe sauce, sugar and basil. Then simmer for about 20 minutes.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente, then drain.
  4. Combine the pasta and turkey mixture; toss and serve.
